Human Review & Edit

Every generated breakdown goes through an interactive editor. Your team reviews, edits, and approves before anything is pushed to Jira. AI assists, humans decide.

How it works

  1. Open a Confluence specification page and click "Generate Breakdown" in the Spec2JIRA panel.
  2. AI analyzes your spec — the pipeline extracts capabilities, groups features, and generates structured work items (~5-10 min).
  3. Review in the Breakdown Editor — edit titles, acceptance criteria, dependencies, and story structure inline.
  4. Push to Jira — approved items are created as epics, stories, and tasks in your Jira project with one click.
